Evidence-Based Treatment Protocols

Standardized protocols developed through extensive clinical research, tailored for specific conditions and patient populations.

Acute Injury Protocol

  • Duration: 20-30 minutes per session
  • Frequency: 2-3 times daily initially
  • Treatment period: 3-7 days
  • Focus: Pain reduction, inflammation control
  • Expected outcome: 50-70% improvement

Chronic Pain Management

  • Duration: 30-45 minutes per session
  • Frequency: Daily for 2-3 weeks
  • Treatment period: 4-8 weeks
  • Focus: Long-term pain relief, function
  • Expected outcome: 60-80% improvement

Post-Surgical Recovery

  • Duration: 25-40 minutes per session
  • Frequency: 1-2 times daily
  • Treatment period: 2-6 weeks
  • Focus: Healing acceleration, scar prevention
  • Expected outcome: 40-60% faster recovery
